Question Evaluation

This is a qualification task for our main job. If you pass the qualification test, we will prompt you to work on our main job later.

In this project, we aim to study the quality of questions generated by automatic models. You will be given the following 4 pieces of information to complete the evaluation.

  • [P] Passage: Passage consisting of multiple sentences with the information required to answer the question.
  • [S] Sentence: The sentence that should contain the answer to the question. This will be boldfaced in [P].
  • [A] Answer: Answer to the question. This is usually an entity that appears in the passage [P].
  • [Q] Generated Question: Question written by our model based on the passage [P] and the answer [A].
We ask you to evaluate [Q] based on the following 4 criteria: (1) Grammaticality, (2) Understandability, (3) Correctness, and (4) Question Complexity.

BEFORE START: Each criterion is explained in our guideline here, so please make sure to read it carefully before start!
